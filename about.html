My Mission

At Wandering Kitsap, my mission is simple: to inspire you to explore the hidden gems and vibrant communities right here in Kitsap County. I believe that adventure doesn't always require a long journey or a hefty budget. Sometimes, the most memorable experiences are just around the corner, waiting to be discovered. My goal is to provide authentic insights, practical tips, and a passion for local exploration that encourages everyone to step out and wander.

I'm dedicated to showcasing the diverse beauty of the Puget Sound region, from serene waterfronts and lush hiking trails to charming small towns and rich historical sites. I strive to highlight budget-friendly activities, ensuring that incredible experiences are accessible to all. Ultimately, I hope to foster a deeper connection between you and the unique spirit of Kitsap, creating lasting memories through genuine local adventures.

Who Am I?

Hey there! I'm Ricardo, the voice and explorer behind Wandering Kitsap. My journey into local exploration began out of a simple curiosity: what amazing places exist right in my own backyard? Living in Kitsap County, I quickly realized there was a wealth of natural beauty, fascinating history, and welcoming communities that often go unnoticed by guidebooks and mainstream travel sites.

I'm not a professional travel writer, just someone with a deep love for discovery and a knack for finding those special, off-the-beaten-path spots. Whether it's tracking down the best viewpoint for a sunset, finding a peaceful trail for a morning hike, or unearthing a quirky local shop, I'm always on the lookout for new adventures. This blog is my way of sharing those discoveries with you, hoping to ignite your own sense of wanderlust.

My philosophy is that great travel doesn't have to be expensive. I'm always on the hunt for free or low-cost activities that offer maximum enjoyment. From picnics with a view to exploring local markets, I believe the best experiences are often those that connect you directly with the heart of a place without draining your wallet. Join me as we uncover the true essence of Kitsap, one adventure at a time!
